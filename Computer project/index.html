Respect School Rules! Every action has consequences and mentoring helps us grow.

Sekolah memiliki wewenang menangani kasus-kasus kesiswaan berdasarkan kebijakan yang berlaku. The school has the authority to handle student-related cases under the following conditions:

Pelanggaran Ringan (Mild Offenses)

Pelanggaran ringan dari tahap pertama sampai keempat ditangani oleh guru mata pelajaran atau wali kelas. Mulai pelanggaran kelima, penanganan dilakukan oleh bagian kesiswaan.

Violation No. Consequence Mechanism
1 - 2 Verbal warning and red slip by teacher/staff Handled by homeroom or subject teacher
3 - 5 Warning, red slip, and community service Handled by Student Affairs
6 - 8 Warning, red slip, community service, and parent call Reported to Vice Principal of Student Affairs
9+ Warning Letter 1-3 and final action by school Serious cases handled by Student Affairs

Pembinaan / Mentoring

  • Regular reporting to the Vice Principal of Student Affairs
  • Mentoring by the homeroom teacher or mentor
  • Counseling by the school counselor

Pelanggaran Sedang (Moderate Offenses)

  1. 1st offense: Verbal warning and library duty
  2. 2nd offense: Notification letter and parent meeting
  3. 3rd offense: Warning Letter II and discipline score reduction

Pelanggaran Berat (Severe Offenses)

Pelanggaran berat ditangani langsung oleh bagian kesiswaan sejak pelanggaran pertama. Severe offenses are handled directly by Student Affairs.

Maksimal Surat Peringatan III (Warning Letter 3) dan siswa dikembalikan kepada orang tua. The final step is returning the student to their parents.
